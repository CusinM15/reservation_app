# 📝 **Teacher Guide — Room Reservation & Exam Scheduling App**

## How to access the app?

Visit [ostc-app.org](https://ostc-app.org). Click `Forgot password?` and enter your school email address. You will receive an email from `os-toneta.cufarja-jesenice@guest.arnes.si`. If you don't receive it, contact the administrator.

## Setting your password

Click the link in the email and set a new password. Requirements:
- At least 5 characters
- At least one uppercase letter
- At least one lowercase letter
- At least one number

## Login

- **Username:** your school email address
- **Password:** the password you set

## Reservations

Available rooms:
- **Computer lab** – one reservation per time slot
- **Wellbeing boat** – one reservation per time slot  
- **Tablets** – up to 28 tablets available, can be shared

### Making a reservation

1. Click on a free time slot
2. Select a room from the dropdown
3. Enter the number of tablets (tablets only)
4. Click Confirm

### Cancelling a reservation

Click on your existing reservation and select Cancel.

## Exam scheduling

1. Go to the **Ocenjevanje** tab
2. Select a class
3. Choose a date and subject
4. Click Confirm

**Limits:**
- Maximum 3 exams per week per class
- Maximum 2 regular written exams per week

## Important

- Forgot your password? Use the "Forgot password" button on the login page
- Check your spam folder if you don't receive the email
- Contact the admin only if you've tried everything
